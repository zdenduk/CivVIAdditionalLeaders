# Civ VI Additional Leaders

Additional civilization and leader content for Sid Meier's Civilization VI.

This repository currently contains `Czechia_Ottokar_Mod`, a Civilization VI mod that adds Czechia as a playable civilization led by Ottokar II, with an additional Czechia variant for Matthias Corvinus.

## Contents

- **Civilization:** Czechia
- **Primary leader:** Ottokar II
- **Additional leader option:** Matthias Corvinus leading Czechia
- **Civilization ability:** Ostsiedlung
- **Unique improvement:** Brewery
- **Unique unit:** Hussite War Wagon

## Czechia

### Civilization Ability: Ostsiedlung

Non-capital Czech cities founded on Hills start with 3 Population.

After the Nationalism civic is unlocked, those Ostsiedlung cities receive a -5 Loyalty penalty each turn.

### Leader Ability: The Iron and Golden King

Ottokar II receives:

- +30% Production toward Heavy Cavalry units
- +30% Production toward Commercial Hubs

### Unique Improvement: Brewery

The Brewery is a Czech unique improvement unlocked with the Guilds civic.

It must be built by a Builder on Wheat near Fresh Water and provides:

- +1 Gold
- +1 Food
- +0.5 Housing
- 1 Beer, a unique Luxury resource
- +1 Faith for each adjacent Holy Site
- +1 Gold for each adjacent Commercial Hub or Harbor
- +1 Food after Mass Production
- +2 Gold after Chemistry

### Unique Unit: Hussite War Wagon

The Hussite War Wagon is a Czech unique ranged unit unlocked with the Reformed Church civic.

It is stronger than the Crossbowman, weaker than the Field Cannon, has increased defensive Combat Strength, and upgrades into the Field Cannon.

## Matthias Corvinus for Czechia

The mod also adds a Czechia-specific Matthias Corvinus leader configuration. This version uses Matthias Corvinus's existing Raven King leader ability and exposes the Black Army alongside Czechia's unique items in the leader selection screen.

## Requirements

This mod declares dependencies on:

- Civilization VI: Rise and Fall
- Civilization VI: Gathering Storm

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Copy the `Czechia_Ottokar_Mod` folder into your Civilization VI Mods directory.
3. Start Civilization VI.
4. Enable `Czechia_Ottokar_Mod` in Additional Content.

Common Mods directories:

- Windows: `Documents\My Games\Sid Meier's Civilization VI\Mods`
- macOS: `~/Library/Application Support/Sid Meier's Civilization VI/Mods`
